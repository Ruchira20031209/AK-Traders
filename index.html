About A K Traders

Our journey from a small hardware store to a leading construction materials supplier

Building Trust Since 2020

A K Traders was founded with a simple mission: to provide high-quality construction materials with honest pricing and exceptional customer service. What started as a small neighborhood hardware store has grown into one of the region's most trusted suppliers of construction materials.

We take pride in our extensive inventory that includes everything from basic hardware to specialized construction materials. Our knowledgeable staff can help you find exactly what you need for your project, whether you're a professional contractor or a DIY homeowner.

Our commitment to quality extends beyond our products. We believe in building lasting relationships with our customers, offering expert advice, and standing behind everything we sell.
